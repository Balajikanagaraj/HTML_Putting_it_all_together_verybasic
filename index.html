Welcome to Learn About Earth

1. The Earth's core is as hot as the surface of the sun, reaching temperatures of up to 6000°C. Despite this, the Earth's crust remains cool and protects us from the intense heat.

2. About 70% of the Earth's surface is covered in water, yet only about 1% of that water is drinkable. This makes fresh water one of the most precious resources on Earth.

3. Earth has an incredibly powerful magnetic field, which acts as a shield against harmful cosmic radiation and solar winds. Without it, life on Earth would be impossible.

Top 3 facts about Earth

  1. The Earth’s rotation is gradually slowing
  2. Earth is at the center of the universe
  3. Earth has a powerful magnetic field
